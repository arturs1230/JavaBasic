Donald Trump to run for president in 2016

Mr Trump, a Republican, has never formally run for president before, but has often talked about it. "I am officially running for president of the United States and we are going to make our country great again," he told supporters at New York's Trump Towers on Fifth Avenue. He said his fortune would allow him to be an effective president. "Our country is in serious trouble. We don't have victories anymore," he said.
